Standard Learning Curriculum

Our metrics follow structured academic benchmarks from Kindergarten to 9th Grade to ensure mastery of core conceptual frameworks.

🐣 Kindergarten Foundation +

Numbers and Counting

  • Count objects up to 20
  • Identify and write numbers 0 to 20
  • Understand "one more" and "one less"

Sorting, Ordering, & Shapes

  • Classify objects by color, size, or shape
  • Identify squares, circles, triangles, and rectangles
  • Compare measurable tracking size (heavier, taller)
🎒 Lower Elementary (Grades 1 - 3) +

Grade 1 Focus

  • Addition and subtraction word problems within 20
  • Tell and write time in hours and half-hours
  • Counting patterns to 120 and place value (tens/ones)

Grade 2 Focus

  • Two-digit and three-digit addition/subtraction
  • Foundations of multiplication (arrays and equal groups)
  • Read bar graphs, line plots, and use standard currency

Grade 3 Focus

  • Mastering multiplication and division facts up to 100
  • Introduction to fractions (identifying parts of a whole)
  • Calculating basic Area and Perimeter tracking metrics
📘 Upper Elementary (Grades 4 - 5) +

Grade 4 Operations

  • Multi-digit multiplication and long division structures
  • Equivalent fractions, comparing fractions, and mixed numbers
  • Decimals tracking: notation and metric system conversions

Grade 5 Mastery

  • Add, subtract, multiply, and divide complex fractions
  • Volume calculations of rectangular prisms
  • Graphing points on a coordinate plane axis system
📐 Middle School Foundations (Grades 6 - 8) +

Grade 6 Ratios & Expressions

  • Understanding ratios, unit rates, and percentages
  • Dividing fractions by fractions cleanly
  • Evaluating single-variable expressions and inequalities

Grade 7 Proportions & Integers

  • Proportional relationships and constant of proportionality
  • Operations with positive and negative rational numbers
  • Solving multi-step real-world linear equations

Grade 8 Pre-Algebra Tracks

  • Linear functions, slope ($\mathit{m}$), and $\mathit{y}$-intercept formulas
  • The Pythagorean Theorem calculations and radical evaluations
  • Systems of linear equations tracking parameters
🚀 High School Track (Grade 9 / Algebra 1) +

Advanced Functions & Equations

  • Solving and graphing systems of linear equations & inequalities
  • Factoring polynomials, quadratics, and identifying trinomial roots
  • Evaluating non-linear exponential and radical function models

Data Analysis & Concepts

  • Analyzing data distribution models (Standard Deviation, Outliers)
  • Arithmetic and geometric progression tracking frameworks
  • Logical proof parameters for high school algebraic systems
